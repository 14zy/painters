Art Challenge








What's going on here?

We offer you to have some fun by playing the art history game!

How does it work?

You need to guess the artists who made famous paintings.
You win if you manage to guess 10 painters in a row.

What’s the use of it?

Play in your free time and very soon you’ll be able to tell Claude Monet from Edouard Manet at a glance, let alone Alfred Sisley :)
