Art Challenge

About

Art Challenge is an ediucaional free game that helps you to learn more about fine arts.

Just play at your free time and after 2 weeks you will level up your recognition of paintings to the whole new level.

Original game was made by Ruben Babaev and Anna Plotkina in Rostov-on-Don, Russia, 2013.
