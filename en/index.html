What's going on here?

You are proposed to entertain yourself by playing the game on knowledge of the history of art.

How does it work?

You need to guess the painters.
You will win if you can guess 10 pictures in a row.

What good can it do?

Play in your free time, and very soon you'll easily differentiate Claude Monet from Edouard Manet at a glance, and certainly from Alfred Sisley :)
